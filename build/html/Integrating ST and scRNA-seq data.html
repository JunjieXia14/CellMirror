Integrating ST adn scRNA-seq Data

In this tutorial, we show how to apply CellMirror to integrate ST and scRNA-seq data. As an example, we use the ST data and paired scRNA-seq data of breast cancer patient CID44971 from Wu, et al. 2021., including 1,162 spots and 7,986 cells.
